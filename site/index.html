How it works

  1. Fill in your city and country
  2. Choose a theme from the gallery below
  3. Click "Generate Map Poster"
  4. You'll be redirected to GitHub to submit an issue (requires GitHub login)
  5. Our automated workflow will generate your poster
  6. You'll receive a comment with a private Gist link to download your poster
